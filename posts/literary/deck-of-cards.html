Deck of Cards

By Kyla Chua
Mon, October 19, 2020

Shuffle, slide, pick, keep, and cover
Gather then organize in order
A piece of some paper or plastic sheet
54 cards, 2 colors and some on repeat

A heart, diamond, spade, and clover
King, Queen, Jack, and we’re sober
Playing games and drinking cups
Broken hearts and skipping naps

Guessing, thinking, in a snap it’s over
Thought you were a pair, I picked a joker
Sometimes it’s red then it’s black
I thought it’s a win, I guess it’s not

Alas, two, three, four, five to ten
Looking back and wondering then
Now all the cards unfold
Maybe it’s time to lose my hold

Cards are thin sheets of something
Easy to bend and fold while playing
Shuffle, slide, keep, and then cover
Would you like to start over?
