Quietude

By Ronah Ceniza Rollan
Reuploaded: Tue, October 13, 2020

Evolution may have wired us to make connections.
Like how all these organic materials in me are linked.
Connections that we intend and don't intend
Connections we keep and break.

Amidst all these mixed faces in the crowd
Like how heterogeneous blood is,
Being alone doesn't define loneliness
And loneliness doesn't define being alone.

Being connected doesn't always look like
Being one with the crowd
Nor being surrounded with companions
Sometimes, it looks like being connected with your inner self and nothing less.

All the motions that seems like uncontrollable bumping electrons
All the noise that seems to be more than 80 dB
I feel like an atom forced to jump to a higher energy level
it was too agitating, an escape velocity is all I need.

My heart longs for white noise and calmness
The oscillating and crashing sound of the sea's blue waves
To flow with the breath of the cave's stalagmites.
To stare at the lovely celestial bodies under the pitch-dark sky
