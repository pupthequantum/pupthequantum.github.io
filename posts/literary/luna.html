Literary

Luna

By Aeybruh
Mon, October 19, 2020

Underneath the crescent moon

The nostalgic song of melody starts to overthrow

You held my bristly hand as we gazed the night sky

I kissed your forehead and started to cry

I reminisce the aroma of pills that cleave up on your shirt

And the dark circles in your dismal optics

As the crystal liquid descends over your cheeks

I remember you said you’ll never leave

I recall the repugnant smell of the hospital hallway

And the echoes of my heels that stamp abroad

The plangent sound of electrocardiograph behind

That caused my heart to burst as I saw your morbid demise

Underneath the crescent moon,

I’ll never hear he melody of your laugh again

Nor the taste of your lips and your crescent hip

Everything will just be a memory

That one of my luminaries was taken away from me.
