We humans have come a long way since our first appearance 100,000 years ago. Technology came and went, from simple tools to complex machinery. The concept of society, of humans gathering together to coexist, raised civilizations from valleys and riverbanks. We have learned to harness and utilize energy, allowing us to do things outside our biological capabilities.
