Education Response in the Age of COVID

By Faye Marie Ugot
Thu, October 15, 2020

With the current pandemic in the backdrop of the global stage, the education system is forced to shift to the ‘new normal’ mode of learning, paving the way not only to innovations, but inevitably to its twin complications as well. We now find ourselves wedged in a unique position between the challenge of seeking efficient ways of learning and teaching whilst dispelling truth deniers, pervasive myths, and willful ignorance in this era of uncertainty.
